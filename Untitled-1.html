Nelson Mandela

A man guided with hope. Not fear.

Below is a list of some of his accomplishments:

  • 1950- He became the National President of the ANC youth league
  • 1948- He was a lead opponent of the 1948 Aparthield legislation
  • 1953- He co-founded the first black legal partnership in South-Africa
  • 1961- His speech during Rivonia trial is considered one of the great speeches of the 20th century
  • 1985- NELSON MANDELA PLAYED THE LEADING ROLE IN ENDING APARTHEID
  • 1994- NELSON MANDELA WAS THE FIRST BLACK PRESIDENT OF SOUTH AFRICA
  • - HE FOCUSED ON NATIONAL RECONCILIATION DURING HIS PRESIDENCY

Difficulties break some men but make others. No axe is sharp enough to cut the soul of a sinner who keeps on trying, one armed with the hope that he will rise even in the end.

---Nelson Mandela.
